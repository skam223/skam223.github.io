Witness the seasonal migration of the humpback whales, appreciate the predator of the sea, the shark from the comfort of your seat and be amazed at the frolicking dolphins as the pod surrounds the boat in a playful dance.

Kama Hele Kai gives visitors and locals alike the advantage of seeing the raw power of the sea from the safety of our vessel.
