Boat

The Nani Kai, meaning “beautiful ocean” in Hawaiian, is 30 feet long and locally built. She is made for the Hawaiian waters and designed for all types of fishing.

30’ fbg Sport Fisher Hull: Banana Patch Local built Engine: Caterpillar 3208 210HP strait shaft speed 20k max

US Coast Guard safety equipped.

Children under 12 years and non swimmers life jacket are available.
